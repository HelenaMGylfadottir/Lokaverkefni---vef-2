We offer a variety of activities during the week! If any of these interest you, all you've gotta do is attend! no need to sign up or even pay, these are done for fun and for educational purposes

Activity Ages Duration Description
METAMORPHOSIS all ages 10:00am - 11:10am Ever wanted to learn more about the life cycle of butterflies and metamorphosis? We offer a colorful presentation for everyone to enjoy going into just that!
ARTS AND CRAFTS all ages 2:00pm - 4:30pm Come learn about bugs and create some beautiful art, including colorful butterflies, beetles and more!
HISSING COCKROACHES all ages 5:00pm - 6:00pm Would you like to learn about hissing cockroaches? Well now's your chance! Learn fascinating facts, get and upclose look at them, and if you're brave enough, you can even hold one!
